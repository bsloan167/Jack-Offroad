About Me

Jack Sloan

Offroad Enthusiast

Advocating for Environmental Conservation and Supporting the Sport


My name is Jack Sloan, and I am an environmentally-conscious offroad enthusiast, not to be confused with a tree hugger. My journey began in 2006 with a 2 door 1993 Ford Explorer that has turned into a capable offroad machine. I used to be the president of the West Virginia Jeep Club but had never owned a Jeep.

Goals

  • Drive a SASD trac cross-country to Moah, UT in the post-Covid-19 world.
  • Build my internet following via YouTube and Instagram for Jack Offroad so more people can watch me do dumb things.
  • Open an offroad shop called Jack Offroad to continue my brand.
  • TBC

Activities

I enjoy land conservation, doing no harm, and leaving nothing behind. I've participated in and spearheaded several clean-up efforts at local offroad locations as well as advocating for my sport. I had the pleasure of participating in Ultimate Adventure 2015 and hosting 3 Appalachian Adventures in my home state of WV.
